About Me

I’m Swathi Kumar, a 6th semester Computer Science student at PES University EC Campus, Bengaluru. I work at the intersection of IoT, cybersecurity, big data, AI and Cloud — and I believe technology should solve real problems for real people.

My journey started at 15, when I built an app to address menstrual hygiene inequality in rural India and reached the global stage of the Technovation Challenge. Since then I’ve gone from MIT App Inventor to AWS services, Apache Kafka, and LLM-driven architectures.

I’ve interned as a Research Intern at CCNCS PESU, where I worked on FailSafe (published at ICTCS 2025) and Unified AIOps in collaboration with Corestack. I also manage logistics at Weal.

I’m actively seeking internship and research opportunities in systems, security, and AI — where I can build things that matter and grow fast.
